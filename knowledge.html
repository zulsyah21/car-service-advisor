Routine Perodua Service Schedule

Understand what components are serviced and replaced during Perodua's official standard checkups at key mileage milestones.

10,000 KM (Minor Service)

Primary maintenance check designed to protect engine friction surfaces and inspect safety systems.

  • Engine Oil Replacement (Fully Synthetic)
  • Engine Oil Filter Replacement
  • Drain Plug Gasket Replacement
  • Brake Pad & Fluid inspection
20,000 KM (Medium Service)

Adds cabin air filtration to ensure fresh air quality and full inspection of undercarriage components.

  • Engine Oil, Filter & Gasket
  • Cabin Air Filter replacement
  • Tire Rotation & Wheel Balancing
  • Steering and Suspension joint check
40,000 KM (Major Service)

Critical milestone replacing heavy-duty fluids, spark plugs, and air cleaner to keep engine running efficiently.

  • Engine Oil, Filter & Cabin Filter
  • Spark Plugs replacement (Nickel models)
  • Engine Air Cleaner Filter replacement
  • Transmission Fluid replacement (CVT/AT)
  • Complete Brake Fluid flushing & replacement
80,000 KM (Super Major Service)

Comprehensive replacement of all components including engine coolant, timing belts (if applicable), and drive belts.

  • All 40,000 KM Major Service items
  • Complete Engine Coolant flushing & replacement
  • Drive Belt (Serpentine Belt) replacement
  • Fuel Filter inspection
